About Me

My name is Megan Johnston and this is a little introduction about me. I was born and raised in Gauteng, attending school at Laerskool Rooihuiskraal, Hoërskool Swartkop and Impaq Online. I switched from a government school to an online school after an accident occurred, but it allowed me to focus more on my work without the constant external stimulation. I am someone who is more than eager to learn new things, but I do have to be wary so that I do not fall into a rabbit hole that will suck me in for months.


I am currently in my second year at Stadio for a Bachelors Degree in Web Design and Development, and am thoroughly enjoying it. I have experience in working for long hours, stepping up to the role of a leader through my Class Representative position, organization of physical and digital documents, as well as using my creative brain to make beautiful things. Me and my team won the Hackathon hosted by Stadio and Otinga in 2025, showcasing my ability to work under a tight time crunch, albeit with the side effect of being out of commission for the next day or two.
